Apple Wallet Pass Images
=======================

Add the following images to this folder:

- icon.png   (required, 29x29 px)
- logo.png   (recommended, ~150x150 px)

You may also add other images as needed (see Apple Wallet documentation).

For best results, use PNG format and follow Apple's image guidelines:
https://developer.apple.com/documentation/walletpasses/images_in_passes
